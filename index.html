About this Site


Welcome to this site

The Citizen Digital/Starting Point is a project that takes an interesting look at some social justice issues in the Portland Metropolatian area. According to The Atlantic,May 17, 2016, "Can Portland Avoid Repeating San Francisco's Mistakes?", "the city got "discovered, people started flocking here, the tech companies came, and Portland became more expensive. Oregon has been the top destination for people to move to forthree years in a row."

Now, housing prices are skyrocketing... more people move in and new high-rises and apartment complexes go up. Apartment rents are risig at an annualized rate of 14 percent, one of th largest increases in the country.

We encourage you to visit the site, read about the team that developed this social justice app for ease of use. Find out how you can get involved with the some of the most current issues happening now in Portland, share your voice and make an impact.

Please share this site with your friends, family, colleagues and your community.

What the user can do on this site

On this site the user has the opportunity to voice their concerns, issues and/or possible solution. With one click the user's voice will be shared on Twitter for others in and out of the Portland area to read and support.

Possible uses for this site

The users visting the site should have their own personal Twitter account in order to post their comments.The users are encouraged to share this site with others, start the conversations and become actively engaged.
